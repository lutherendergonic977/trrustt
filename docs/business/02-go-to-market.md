# Go-To-Market Strategy

## 1. Target Audience

### Primary Personas

| Persona | Role | Pain Point | Value Prop |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Priya, BI Analyst** | Creates 5-10 dashboards/week for stakeholders | Repetitive work, DAX is hard, slow to deliver | 10x faster dashboard creation |
| **Marcus, BI Consultant** | Builds dashboards for multiple clients | Time = money, need consistency, client revisions | Deliver faster, charge more projects |
| **Sarah, Data Team Lead** | Manages team of 5 analysts | Quality varies, onboarding slow, DAX errors common | Standardize quality, upskill team |
| **Tom, CTO at SaaS Co** | Needs embedded analytics for product | Expensive to build, hard to maintain | White-label OEM, embed in product |

### Secondary Personas

| Persona | Role | Opportunity |
|---|---|---|
| **Alex, Power BI Trainer** | Teaches Power BI/DAX | Use as teaching tool, affiliate revenue |

## 2. Positioning

### Brand Positioning Statement

> *For Power BI professionals who spend too much time on repetitive dashboard creation and DAX writing, IntelliDashboard Builder is the AI-powered automation platform that learns your data, auto-generates production-ready DAX, and creates stunning dashboards — so you can focus on insights, not implementation.*

### Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | Our Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| Power BI Copilot | Basic AI features in PBI Service | Deeper DAX, configurable, works offline, image-to-dashboard |
| Tabular Editor | DAX/TOM scripting | AI-powered, full dashboard generation, not just scripting |
| Zebra BI | Custom visuals | Full pipeline, not just visuals |
| Narrative Science | NLG for data | We generate dashboards, not just text |
| ChatGPT/Claude | General AI | Purpose-built for Power BI, schema-aware, validates output |

### Unique Selling Propositions

1. **100% Configurable** — Nothing hard-coded. Every behavior adjustable.
2. **Image-to-Dashboard** — Upload a sketch, get a working dashboard.
3. **DAX Engine** — Generate, validate, explain, and self-correct DAX.
4. **MCP-Native** — Connect to any MCP-compatible AI tool.
5. **Headless/CLI** — Works without Power BI Desktop open.
6. **White-Label Ready** — Rebrand and resell as your own product.
